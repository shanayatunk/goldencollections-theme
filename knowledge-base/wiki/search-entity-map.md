## Regional Name Strategy

Golden Collections should intentionally map regional names for the same ornament or deity accessory. This matters especially for deity jewellery, where the same piece may be searched in Telugu, Tamil, Kannada, Hindi, Sanskrit-derived, and English terms.

Examples to model:

- Deity crown: mukut, kireedam, kirita, crown.
- Waist belt: vaddanam, oddiyanam, kamarband, kati sutra.
- Ear chain: mattal, matil, mattel.
- Forehead ornament: nethi chutti, maang tikka, tikka.
- Ankle bells: ghungroo, salangai.
- Vishnu marks: namam, thiruman, tilak.

Each deity accessory page should identify whether the item is deity-specific, compatible with multiple deities, or common across many god/goddess idols.
